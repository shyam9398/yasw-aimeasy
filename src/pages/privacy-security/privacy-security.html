Privacy and Policies

Your learning data should stay protected and purposeful.

AIIENS Edu is designed to store only the information needed to run student, creator, and admin workflows. Access is role-based and data is used only for learning, content delivery, platform support, and academic management.

Data Use

Profile, subject, and progress data is used to personalize the learning experience.

Access Control

Admin, sub-admin, creator, and student areas are separated by role-specific flows.

Security

When connected to Supabase, app data syncs through configured project credentials.

Information We Use

AIIENS Edu may use profile details, role information, selected branch, semester, subject progress, uploaded content records, and learning activity data. This information helps the platform show the right subjects, track academic progress, and keep content organized for students and creators.

We avoid collecting unnecessary information. Data should support the learning experience, administrative control, or platform reliability.

Role-Based Access

Students, content creators, admins, and sub-admins should only access the tools required for their responsibilities.

Content Responsibility

Uploaded notes, videos, PYQs, and important questions should be relevant, accurate, and appropriate for academic use.

Account Safety

Users should protect login access, avoid sharing credentials, and report suspicious activity as early as possible.

Data Sync

When backend sync is enabled, app data is stored through the configured Supabase project and related access settings.

Platform Policies

AIIENS Edu is intended for academic preparation and learning support. Users should not upload harmful, misleading, copyrighted, or unrelated content. Admins and content creators are responsible for keeping curriculum material clean, useful, and aligned with student needs.

These policies may evolve as the platform grows. The priority will remain the same: protect users, keep learning data meaningful, and maintain a trustworthy academic environment.
